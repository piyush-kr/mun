25th, 26th and 27th November

RECOGNISE, READAPT, REAFFIRM

The change in global demographics over the last two years due to the pandemic marks a tectonic shift in human nature, placing it as a major event of the millennia. The struggle for survival has forced economies to adopt countermeasures which have affected the rural and urban poor globally. The sociological and psychological aspects when critically analysed give us an idea of the sheer mental fatigue that has taken over the society at large.

Therefore, we believe the 3 R's of the post-pandemic world order would attempt to guide the new world into another humble beginning.

To RECOGNISE the problems, to READAPT according to the circumstances and to REAFFIRM the ones affected for a better tomorrow is the mission for this year's conference.

#4Future

AIMUN has collaborated with United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in the MUN Refugee Challenge and the Best Delegate (USA). The Refugee Challenge focuses on discussions regarding various refugee crises and the best resolutions would be selected by the UNHCR and shared with global policymakers. This provides an international opportunity for the delegates of this conference to reach out to the world in a practical manner.
