What is MUN?

A Model United Nations or MUN is a simulation of the United Nations with a similar structure and mode of operation. Its main objectives are similar to those of the UN, which include fostering conversation on a variety of agendas in different committees and identifying solutions through discussions, debates, diplomacy and Resolutions.

About AIMUN

AIMUN is a youth-led non-profit organization that focuses on providing educational opportunities regarding diplomacy and public-speaking. The team is entirely dedicated to its objective of being a leading organisation that encourages discussions and initiates conversations of global importance. To be the medium through which leaders of tomorrow step into the world of conflicting realities to resolve and reintroduce a society of peace is our fundamental goal. AIMUN, therefore, acknowledges the role of youth in peace building and societal construction towards a secured future and to motivate these young leaders towards greater resolutions and healthier decisions.

Our Highlights

AIMUN has collaborated with United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in the MUN Refugee Challenge and the Best Delegate (USA). The Refugee Challenge focuses on discussions regarding various refugee crises and the best resolutions would be selected by the UNHCR and shared with global policymakers. This provides an international opportunity for the delegates of this conference to reach out to the world in a practical manner.
